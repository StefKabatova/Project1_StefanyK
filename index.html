Trivia

Welcome to MAD Trivia, a brain-challenges game that testes your knowledge about the world.

How it Works:

  • There is a total of 3 questions in each round and a total of 3 rounds per category.
  • Each correct question is worth 100 points. Top score is of 1200 points.
  • As you move up to round 3, each incorrect question is -20 points.
